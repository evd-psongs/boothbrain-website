# Privacy Policy for BoothBrain

**Last Updated:** November 14, 2025

## Introduction

BoothBrain ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

**Account Information**
- Email address
- Password (encrypted)
- Display name
- Profile information

**Business Data**
- Inventory items (products, prices, descriptions, photos)
- Sales transactions
- Event details
- Customer information (if you choose to collect it)
- Payment records

### Automatically Collected Information

**Device Information**
- Device type and model
- Operating system version
- Unique device identifiers
- App version

**Usage Data**
- App features used
- Session duration
- Crash reports and error logs
- Performance data

**Biometric Data**
- We use Face ID/Touch ID/Fingerprint for authentication
- Biometric data is processed locally on your device only
- We never store or transmit your biometric data

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- **Provide Services:** Operate and maintain the app functionality
- **Account Management:** Create and manage your account
- **Data Sync:** Synchronize your data across your devices
- **Payment Processing:** Process subscription payments via Stripe
- **Customer Support:** Respond to your questions and support requests
- **Improve Services:** Analyze app usage to improve features and performance
- **Security:** Protect against fraud and unauthorized access
- **Legal Compliance:** Comply with legal obligations

## Data Storage and Security

### Where We Store Data

- **Supabase:** Your account data, inventory, and sales records are stored securely on Supabase servers (industry-standard encryption)
- **Local Device:** App data is cached locally on your device for offline access
- **Stripe:** Payment information is processed and stored by Stripe (PCI-DSS compliant)

### Security Measures

- All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using SSL/TLS
- Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text
- Biometric authentication adds an extra layer of security
- Regular security audits and updates

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

We may share your information with:

**Service Providers**
- **Supabase:** Database and authentication services
- **Stripe:** Payment processing (they have their own privacy policy)
- **Firebase:** Crash reporting and analytics (anonymized)

**Legal Requirements**
We may disclose your information if required by law, subpoena, or other legal process.

**Business Transfers**
If BoothBrain is acquired or merged, your information may be transferred to the new owner.
