## FAQ Content (for Support Page)

### Getting Started

**Q: How do I create an account?**
A: Download BoothBrain from the App Store or Google Play, open the app, and tap "Sign Up." Enter your email and create a password.

**Q: Is there a free trial?**
A: Yes! New users get a [X-day] free trial with full access to all features.

**Q: What devices are supported?**
A: BoothBrain works on iPhone (iOS 13+) and Android phones (Android 8+).

### Inventory Management

**Q: How do I add products?**
A: Tap the "+" button on the Inventory screen, enter product details, and save. You can also import products via CSV.

**Q: Can I add product photos?**
A: Yes! Tap on a product, then tap "Add Photo" to upload an image from your camera or photo library.

**Q: How do I import inventory from a spreadsheet?**
A: Go to Inventory → Menu → Import CSV. Download the template, fill it with your products, and upload.

### Sales & Payments

**Q: What payment methods are supported?**
A: Cash, card, Venmo, PayPal, and custom payment methods you configure.

**Q: How do I process a sale?**
A: Start a session, add items to the cart, select payment method, and complete checkout.

**Q: Can I accept partial payments?**
A: Yes! You can split payments across multiple methods during checkout.

### Events & Sessions

**Q: What's the difference between an event and a session?**
A: An event is a market/fair you're attending. A session is when you're actively selling at that event.

**Q: Can I track multiple events?**
A: Yes! Create unlimited events and track sales separately for each.

### Account & Billing

**Q: How do I cancel my subscription?**
A: Go to Settings → Subscription → Cancel Subscription. You can also cancel through your App Store or Google Play account.

**Q: Will I lose my data if I cancel?**
A: Your data is saved for 30 days after cancellation. You can export it anytime before deletion.

**Q: How do I export my data?**
A: Go to Inventory → Menu → Export CSV to download your inventory and sales data.

### Technical Issues

**Q: The app won't load. What should I do?**
A: Try these steps:
1. Check your internet connection
2. Force close and reopen the app
3. Restart your device
4. Check for app updates in the App Store

**Q: I forgot my password. How do I reset it?**
A: Tap "Forgot Password?" on the login screen and follow the email instructions.

**Q: Does BoothBrain work offline?**
A: Yes! You can process sales offline. Data will sync when you reconnect to the internet.
